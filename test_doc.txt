Product Specification: AI Innovation Hub

Title: AI Innovation Hub
Problem Statement: Organizations struggle to track, evaluate, and approve AI project proposals across departments. This product provides a centralized platform for submitting, reviewing, and managing AI use case registrations.
Primary Users: Business analysts, project managers, and innovation leaders across all departments.
Deployment: The platform will be deployed in the US East region.
AI Model: The system uses GPT-4 for intelligent document parsing and conversational AI.
Development Partner: Accenture Digital
Budget: The estimated budget is between $500K and $1M.
